Decision Support Tool - Excel VBA

The project was created with four other team members. This program helps you to plan and execute a project. You are able to see each team member's schedule, search questions, track the progress of the project, and make a cause-and-effect diagram (ishikawa or fishbone).

Budgeting Calendar - Processing

The Budgeting Calendar allows a user to select a range of dates for their budget. Then they can enter specific dates that they expect revenues and expenses. Once everything is entered, the user can view it on a calendar with a summary on the side. There is also a feature to save a picture of the calendar.

Babysitting Nightmare Game - Python

This game takes you on the journey of babysitting. The baby is very smart and mischievous....and also wants to get you fired. So the baby decides to throw food splatters around the kitchen. Your goal is to clean the splatters up before the parents come home. On the bottom is a video demo of the game.

Tranquility App - MIT App Inventor

The Tranquility App was coded using MIT App inventor with three other team members. The app allows users to create an account which lets them access the features of the app. Tranquility allows users to track their mood, takes them through calming exercises, and contact people when they need someone to talk to.

Cellular Automaton - Processing

This program simulates oral health and hygiene. Random food particles appear on the teeth and then plaque starts to grow near it. The food particles and plaque can only be destroyed by the toothpaste particles. But if the plaque stays on the tooth for too long, it will turn into a cavity, where toothpaste can't get rid of it.

  • White cells = healthy teeth
  • Yellow cells = plaque
  • Green cells = food particles
  • Blue cells = water and toothpaste
  • Black cells = cavities
